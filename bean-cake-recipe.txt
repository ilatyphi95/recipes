Ingredients:
 - 3 cups of beans flour
 - 2 tablespoon of pepper
 - 5 cups of lukewarm water
 - 6 tablespoon of vegetable oil
 - 1 tablespoon of salt
 - 3 boiled eggs
 - Seasoning

Directions:
 1. Dice the boiled eggs

 2. In a large bowl, combine the beans flour, water, pepper,vegetable oil and salt.
    Mix throughly until. Then season to taste.

 3. Add the diced eggs and stir briskly.

 4. Share into oven suitable containers of about 300ml.

 5. Bake for 45 to 60 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out
    clean.
